Welcome to Plant Disease Detection

Plant Disease Detection is an AI-powered system that helps identify diseases in plants by analyzing images of leaves. It enables farmers and gardeners to detect plant diseases early and take preventive measures to protect crops.

Features

  • Detects plant diseases from leaf images
  • Image-based disease identification
  • Fast and accurate results
  • Wind speed details
  • AI-powered analysis
  • Helps improve crop health and yield

How It Works

The user uploads an image of a plant leaf. The system analyzes the image using Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning techniques. After processing, it identifies the disease and provides the result along with possible recommendations.

Benefits

  • Early detection of plant diseases
  • Reduces crop losses
  • Improves agricultural productivity
  • Saves time and effort
  • Supports better crop management
